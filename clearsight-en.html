Key Features

📖

AI Text Reader & OCR Scanner

Point your camera at any printed or handwritten text. We use OCR to scan text from books, documents, street signs, and product labels — then read it aloud.

AI Image Description

Take a photo of anything and get an instant, detailed AI description. Perfect as a visual aid for understanding your surroundings.

Book Mode

Scan entire books page by page. ClearSight stitches multiple scans into one continuous audio experience — the ultimate read-aloud listener.

Draw a circle on the screen to focus the AI on a specific area. Read or describe just the part of the image you need.
