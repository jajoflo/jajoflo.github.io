Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

  • Images: When you use the "Capture" feature, the image is temporarily processed to generate the description.
  • Device Identifiers: We collect device identifiers (such as Advertising ID) to serve advertisements and specifically to measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns (installation attribution).
  • App Activity & Analytics: We use Google Firebase Analytics to collect data on how you interact with the App (e.g., screens viewed, buttons clicked, "first open" events). This helps us understand user behavior and improve app stability.
  • Approximate Location: Your IP address may be used by our analytics providers to determine your general location (city/country level) for aggregate reporting.

2. How We Use Information

  • Service Provision: Images are sent to our AI provider (Google Gemini API) for analysis. We do not permanently store your photos on our servers; they are discarded immediately after processing.
  • Analytics & Improvement: We use analytics data to identify bugs, optimize performance, and understand which features are most used.
  • Advertising & Marketing: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. We also link usage data (such as app installation and first-open events) with Google Ads to optimize our marketing and prevent fraud (bot filtering).

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is shared with the following third-party service providers only to the extent necessary for the App's functionality and improvement:

  • Google Gemini API: For image processing (Transient/Ephemeral).
  • Google Firebase & Google Analytics: For usage analysis and crash reporting.
  • Google Ads & AdMob: For advertising delivery and campaign measurement.

4. Data Retention

Analytics and crash log data are retained by our service providers (Google) for a limited period to allow for historical analysis and are then automatically deleted in accordance with their data retention policies.

5. Your Consent

By using the App, you consent to the processing of your data as described in this policy.
